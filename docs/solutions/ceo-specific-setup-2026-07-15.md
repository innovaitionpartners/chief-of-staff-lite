# Keep Chief of Staff Lite CEO-specific

## Lesson

When auditing or evolving Chief of Staff Lite, preserve its CEO-focused positioning. Do not generalize its language to a multi-leader audience merely because the template can be technically portable.

## Required installer context

The installer should explicitly collect the CEO's name, company, strategic mandate, priorities, decision rights, key stakeholders, information sources, and briefing preferences. It writes that context into the marked configuration block of the CEO's local daily skill so the brief feels like the CEO's own operating system while remaining tool-agnostic and read-only.

## Trigger

Apply this rule whenever changing the installer questions, generated skill template, executive-brief terminology, or behavioral evaluations.
