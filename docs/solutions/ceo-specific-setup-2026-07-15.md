# Keep Chief of Staff Lite CEO-specific

## Lesson

When auditing or evolving Chief of Staff Lite, preserve its CEO-focused positioning. Do not generalize its language to a multi-leader audience merely because the template can be technically portable.

## Required setup context

The profile setup should explicitly collect the CEO's name, company, CEO role, strategic mandate, priorities, decision rights, key stakeholders, information sources, and briefing preferences. The profile should use that context to make the daily brief feel like the CEO's own operating system, while remaining tool-agnostic and read-only.

## Trigger

Apply this rule whenever changing the skill's positioning, profile template, executive-brief terminology, catalog entry, or behavioral evaluations.
